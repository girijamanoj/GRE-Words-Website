The Aaron Martin House is a historic house at 786 Moody Street in Waltham, Massachusetts.::The 2-1/2 story wood frame house was built in the 1890s by Waltham Watch Company employee and real estate speculator Aaron Martin.::It is a particularly well-preserved local example of Queen Anne styling.::It has a variety of projections, gables, and porches, in a manner typical of the style, as well as a 3-1/2 story tower with a bell-shaped roof.::Its porches are elaborately decorated with gingerbread woodwork.::The house was built in a Queen Anne style and was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1989.::
