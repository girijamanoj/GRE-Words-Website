Princesa do Sul Futebol Clube, commonly known as Princesa do Sul, is a Brazilian football club based in Floriano, Piauí state.::The club was founded on December 1, 2001.::Princesa do Sul Futebol Clube play their home games at Estádio Tibério Barbosa Nunes, nicknamed Tiberão.::The stadium has a maximum capacity of 4,500 people.::
