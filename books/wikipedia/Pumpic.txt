Pumpic is a parental mobile monitoring service launched in 2012. The company develops and supports mobile and tablet monitoring software compatible with iOS and Android platforms. Pumpic application was initially created for parents or guardians to monitor and control activities on their kids' devices. It has many customer support resources and a variety of alerts and restrictions parents can set on their child's device. The monitoring and controlling features are available from a PC, smartphone or tablet after installing the application on the target device. Pumpic application has more than 20 features.
