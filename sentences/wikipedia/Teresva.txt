Teresva (Ukrainian: Тересва, Hungarian: Taracköz, German: Theresiental) is an urban-type settlement in Tiachiv Raion (district) of Zakarpattia Oblast (region) in western Ukraine.::It is situated at the confluence of the rivers Tisza and Teresva.::The town's population was 7,554 at the 2001 Ukrainian Census.::Current population: 7,617 (2016 est.::).::
