Showcase is a compilation album by American rock and roll singer Buddy Holly.::The album was released as an LP record in both mono and stereo formats in May 1964 (see 1964 in music).::Showcase was Buddy Holly's fourth posthumously released album and the third album to feature previously unreleased material.::The original recordings were overdubbed by the Fireballs in 1963.::The album was released on CD for the first time in 1992 by Castle Communications in the UK.::Side ASide B::
