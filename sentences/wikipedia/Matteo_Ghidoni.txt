Matteo Ghidoni (born in Florence around 1626,  died in Padua on 24 January 1689) was an Italian painter of the Baroque period.::Born in Florence, he was also known as Matteo dei Pitocchi, due to his dotty and flashy brushstrokes.::He painted burlesque genre paintings, similar to those of Alessandro Magnasco.::He died in Padua.::
